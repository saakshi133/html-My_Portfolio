The Best Movies According to Saakshi

My top 3 movies of all time

Veer-Zaara

This movie is about two people from two different countries who eventually fall in love with each other.

Fanna

This movie is a love story on a blind girl who falls in love with a tour guide.

Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Gham

This movie is a family entertainer which shows Indian culture and love among the families.
